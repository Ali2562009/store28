Who We Are

Future City Maktaba is a local family-run store in Haykstep Future City, El Shorouk. We specialize in providing school books and essential supplies like notebooks, sharpeners, and other stationery items.

How We Work

- Books are available **by order** only.
- Delivery is limited to **Future City, El Shorouk**.
- We focus on serving students with affordable and high-quality school tools.
